WHO WE ARE

We’re a trusted international telecommunications company dedicated to providing our customers with a convenient, hassle-free way to communicate without borders. Live like a local, say goodbye to roaming, and get everything you want out of a SIM card.

Why V-Tell?

We’ve created the ultimate unlimited communications system by erasing borders among countries via a roam-free network allowing customers to access voice, SMS, and worldwide internet service.

Our communications system features:

  • Our own technological infrastructure, including HLR, GMSC, and GGSN
  • Partnership by contract with the largest GMNO
  • A license agreement with the GSM Association
  • Membership with the ITU

With a V-Tell SIM card, you’ll be able to get an unlimited amount of phone numbers from any country, which will remain permanently active on a single device. Subscribers may also transfer their existing personal phone number from any mobile operator and country to their V-Tell SIM Card.

With a local phone number, V-Tell subscribers can run their businesses from various locations around the world. Our service ensures that no matter what country subscribers are located in, their calls will always appear from the called party’s local number.
