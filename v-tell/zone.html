V-Tell’s Roaming-Free Mobile Systems have dissolved country borders, allowing V-Tell Subscribers to make calls, access internet and send SMS messages from any part of the world, as if they were in their home country.

A solo V-Tell SIM Card allows Subscribers to have an unlimited amount of permanently active phone numbers from over 77 countries. V-Tell’s quality of service is guaranteed by its own personal telecommunication infrastructure and partnerships with world’s leading mobile network operators.

For people who live outside their home country, V-Tell’s services bring unique values, compared to local mobile network operators. Relatives and friends located in different countries do not have to make international calls if the V-Tell Subscriber has activated those countries local phone numbers.
